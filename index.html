about
I'm currently a political columnist at Truthout. Before that, I was an associate editor and reporter at The Intercept. I've assisted with freelance fact-checking and investigative research for various publications, including Business Insider, New York, and The Drift.

My reporting has been cited extensively by news outlets (like ProPublica), academic journals (such as the The Cambridge Journal of Law, Politics, and Art), and prominent nonprofit orgs (including the Natural Resources Defense Council and Beyond Plastics).

After graduating from Pomona College in 2020, I got my start in journalism as an intern at Los Angeles Magazine, which I juggled while completing DoorDash deliveries and SEO content writing gigs. I was born in Texas and raised in North Carolina.
